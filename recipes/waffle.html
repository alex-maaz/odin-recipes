Waffle

Breakfast becomes an occasion when you make waffles.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Steps:

  1. In a large bowl, stir together flour, baking powder, sugar and salt
  2. Add milk, eggs and oil and mix well.
  3. Spray preheated waffle iron with non-stick cooking spray.
  4. Pour mix onto hot waffle iron.
  5. Cook until golden brown.
